How to Get Involved

If you would like to get involved, please look at the openings below and apply using the Google Form below:

  • Slideshow Researcher and Editor: Help find content for the slides and create them.
  • Paper Researcher and Editor: Help find content and write papers.
  • Newsletter Writer: Help write newsletters, pick content, and feature stories.
  • Career Spotlight: Find careers to highlight weekly and research influential people in those fields.
  • Outreach: Reaching out to organizations to explore partnerships (position in development).
